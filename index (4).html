Our Mission

No Patient Should Be Denied Treatment

To implement government and private insurance panels in all hospitals across Jind and nearby areas, so that no patient is deprived of treatment due to financial constraints.

Ayushman Bharat in Every Hospital

Ensuring Ayushman Bharat Yojana (Ayushman Card) is available and accepted in every empanelled hospital.
